Methodology

  1. Venues are curated for practical research navigation across CS, AI/ML, and Data Science.
  2. Priority is given to highly recognized flagship conferences and journals, then expanded to strong adjacent venues.
  3. Area mappings are taxonomy-based and intended to support discovery, not to impose rigid field boundaries.
  4. Calls for papers may be labeled Confirmed, Estimated, or TBA.
  5. Edition logs and latest issue logs are structured for consistency and public GitHub Pages delivery.

When date certainty is low, the directory intentionally marks deadlines as estimated or TBA rather than presenting doubtful precision.

Important: this project is a curated publishing directory and discovery map, not an official ranking publication or official source for final submission instructions.

Always verify deadlines and submission rules on the official venue website.
